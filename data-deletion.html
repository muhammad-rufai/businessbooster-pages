User Data Deletion Instructions for BUSINESS SOLUTION NG

If you would like to request deletion of your personal data collected by BUSINESS SOLUTION NG, please follow these steps:

  • Contact us by email at info@businessbooster.ng.
  • Provide your account information (e.g., name, email, phone number).
  • We will delete your data from our system within 30 days and confirm via email.

Note: Deletion requests apply only to data collected via our app and services. Some information may be retained as required by law.
